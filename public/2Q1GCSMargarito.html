Leni Robredo:



Objective


Angat buhay is a non-profit organization established by former Vice President Leni Robredo with programs aimed to alleviate poverty and respond to disasters. The organization was established on July 1, 2022 after her term as vice president of the Philippines. Angat Buhay serves to continue the relief activities she started during the pandemic and her term.




Operations


Angat buhay implements programs nationwide, including the following:

Programs Description Example
Public Education

Angat buhay intiates education for the Filipino youth, primarily on literacy and numeracy to ensure they acquire skills fit for employment. They also collaborate with partners to provide learning facilities.
